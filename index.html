About DriveWise

DriveWise is built to make your city smarter, one emoji at a time. 🚦🚧

Traffic congestion is one of the biggest challenges in urban areas — messing with commute times, safety, and even the air we breathe. Our mission? Use data and real-time feedback from you, the community, to fight back.

Our predictive model forecasts traffic conditions up to 15 minutes ahead with about 85% accuracy. Whether it's roadwork, accidents, or a random downpour slowing traffic down, DriveWise lets you drop quick emoji reports to alert others in real-time.
